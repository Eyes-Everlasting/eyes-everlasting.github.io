Eyes Everlasting

This Extention is currently in active development and not yet ready to be released to the public

This is a browser extention based on the well known Shinigami Eyes.
While I really like the extention there have always been some things I wished it supported.
So this extention attempts to address those points.

What differentiates Eyes Everlasting from Shinigami Eyes?

  • More Categories
    • Hostile
    • Friendly
    • Caution
    • Watch
    • Note
    • Override
      • This list can be used to remove the highlighting added by another list in case you disagree with a list sourced online
  • No focus on Anti-Trans/Trans-Friendly
    • Eyes Everlasting was made for any and all marginalized communities that benefit from being given a headsup about people and websites.
  • Local lists
  • shared lists
    • upload your local list anywhere and provide others with a link to add it to their Extention
  • Choosing highlight colors for different Categories
  • Reasons why somebody was added to a list
  • List sorting
    • This can be used to change which list takes priority when it comes to highlight color.

And best of all: Eyes Everlasting offers all this functionality while also giving you the ability to see the highlighting Shinigami Eyes would have given you, which of course you can turn off if you don't want to see them.

What i find important to mention: I believe people have the capacity to change and improve, for that reason entries on lists maintained by me my be moved to other lists over time or removed completely. Forgiveness is hard, especially when there has been a long history of hateful behavior, but it is still important to give people a chance once they have earned it.
I would also like to highly suggest to other people that choose to maintain a list to allow people to show they have changed and reflect that on their lists.

Things I plan to add before releasing the Extention:

  • A list of lists on this website to make finding lists that suit your needs easier
  • An easy way to suggest lists to add to this Website
  • An easy way to report lists that can be found on this website
  • A simple process to add lists found online to the Extention by just clicking a button
  • Proper, solid documentation to make understanding usage of this extention a little easier
  • Translations/An easy way to make translations for the extention
  • Proper design for this website so it's not an eyesore anymore
